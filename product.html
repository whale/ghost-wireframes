When you publish a post, it goes out to a network of millions across Mastodon, Threads, Flipboard, WordPress, and more — through an open protocol called ActivityPub. No proprietary algorithm decides who sees your work. When someone reposts you, their followers see it too. That's how independent publishers get network effects without giving up independence.

This is new. It's still evolving. And it's one of the most exciting things happening on the internet.
